Mars Trips with Pets FAQs

Pets on Mars? Possible or Not?

Absolutely! Your furry friends can join the adventure to Mars with our exclusive pet-friendly trips.

What pets are allowed?

From dogs to cats, rabbits to parrots, we welcome all pets on board for an out-of-this-world experience.

Is it safe for pets?

Rest assured, our Mars trips are designed with pet safety as a top priority, ensuring a comfortable journey for all companions.

How to prepare my pet?

Our expert team will guide you on the best practices to prepare your pet for the interplanetary voyage.

Can pets roam freely?

While on Mars, pets will have designated play areas to explore and enjoy the Martian landscape.
